Our Story

Meet the Team Behind
Nori and Panko

We're a fun, passionate team of veterinarians who believe the best vet visit is the one your pet barely notices — because they're still in their favorite spot: home.

Why We Exist

Vet Care the Way Pets Prefer It

We started Nori and Panko because we kept seeing the same thing: healthy, happy pets turning into anxious, trembling balls of fur the moment they got near a clinic. We thought — what if we just went to them instead?

Our names are inspired by Japanese food — Nori (the seaweed) and Panko (the breadcrumbs) — because we love the idea of names that are warm, a little quirky, and instantly memorable. Just like us.

Today, we're a growing team of licensed veterinarians serving pet families across the Philippines, bringing vaccinations, consultations, spay/neuter procedures, and more — straight to your door.

The Team

The Veterinarians Who Come to You

Our team is made up of licensed, compassionate veterinarians who are serious about pet health — and seriously fun to have around.

👩‍⚕️
Dr. Nori
Lead Veterinarian & Co-Founder

Dr. Nori has a passion for feline medicine and preventive care. Known for her calm energy and gentle hands, she can win over even the most suspicious cats within minutes of arriving. She believes every pet deserves to be treated with the patience and dignity they deserve.

Licensed DVM Feline Specialist PVMA Member
👨‍⚕️
Dr. Panko
Senior Veterinarian & Co-Founder

Dr. Panko specializes in canine health and minor surgical procedures including spay and neuter. What We Believe

Our Core Values

These principles guide every home visit, every diagnosis, and every interaction we have with you and your pet.

🐾
Pets First, Always

Every decision we make is guided by what is best for the animal in front of us. Their comfort, safety, and well-being are our top priority — no shortcuts.

🏡
Home is Where Healing Happens

We firmly believe pets recover better and feel safer at home. Bringing vet care to them isn't just convenient — it's genuinely better medicine.

💬
Honest Communication

We tell you what your pet needs — nothing more, nothing less. No unnecessary upsells, no confusing jargon. Just clear, honest veterinary guidance.

🔬
Evidence-Based Care

Our recommendations are backed by current veterinary science. We stay updated on best practices so your pets benefit from the most effective treatments available.

🤝
Partnership with Owners

You know your pet best. We partner with you to make informed decisions together, respecting your role as your pet's primary caregiver and advocate.

😊
Warmth in Every Visit

We bring genuine warmth to every home we enter. Vet care doesn't have to feel cold or clinical — it can be friendly, reassuring, and even a little fun.
